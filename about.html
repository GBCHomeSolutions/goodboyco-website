Our Story & Values

Good Boy Co. Home Solutions was born from a simple love for Christmas lights and the joy they bring. What started as a college side project for Jake has grown into a local, family‑owned company serving the communities of Texas. Our mission is to spread the joy of Christmas to every neighborhood and build lasting relationships along the way. We pride ourselves on understanding how to help each customer best and we don’t stop until your home shines exactly the way you imagined.

  • We are a local, family‑owned business.
  • We build relationships and tailor our services to your needs.
  • We spread the joy of Christmas to every neighborhood.
  • We are determined to get the job done right and guarantee satisfaction.

Meet the Owners

Jake, Megan & Tex

Jake’s passion for holiday lighting began while he was studying at the Colorado School of Mines. He started a small lighting company in college, delighting his neighborhood with festive displays. After marrying Megan, the couple moved to Texas with their loyal pup Tex and transformed that college project into Good Boy Co. Home Solutions.

Today, Jake and Megan bring that same joy and craftsmanship to every project. They believe that great service starts with great relationships. From your first call to the final twinkle of lights, they treat your home as if it were their own—ensuring every detail is perfect and every customer feels like family.

Caleb & Ben

Caleb met Jake at the Colorado School of Mines, where they were both student‑athletes pursuing mechanical engineering degrees. Bonded by a love of design and a relentless work ethic, Caleb joined the Good Boy Co. journey early on.

Alongside his dog Ben, Caleb brings precision and energy to every installation. He helped Jake take the company from college dream to professional enterprise, and he now focuses on making each customer’s home look its very best. Together, they’re dedicated to building a service company that puts the customer first—every single time.
